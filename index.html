About Our Hospital

Best Emergency Response Hospital in Muzaffarpur, Bihar

Welcome to Code Blue Emergency Hospital, your trusted destination for fast, reliable, and compassionate healthcare services. Available 24/7, we specialize in emergency care, critical care, and general medical services to ensure patients receive immediate attention when they need it most. Led by Dr. Tarique Jamil, our experienced medical team is committed to delivering prompt diagnosis, effective treatment, and continuous support in a safe and patient-centered environment.

We provide expert care for a wide range of medical emergencies, including accidents, injuries, heart-related conditions, breathing difficulties, poisoning, and other urgent health concerns. Equipped with modern facilities and skilled healthcare professionals, we are prepared to respond quickly when every second matters.
